Course we offer

Air University offers a range of courses in fields such as engineering, technology, business, and science, providing students with the skills and knowledge needed for successful careers.

Bachelor of Science

Air University offers a Bachelor of Science degree with specializations in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, Mathematics, and Physics. Each program provides a strong academic foundation and practical skills for future careers. The courses are designed to equip students with essential knowledge and expertise in their chosen fields.

Master of Science

Air University offers a Master of Science degree with specializations in areas such as Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, and Data Science. These programs provide advanced knowledge and research opportunities, preparing students for leadership roles and specialized careers in their fields. The courses are designed to enhance expertise and practical skills for professional success.

Doctor of Philosophy

Air University offers a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in fields like Computer Science, Engineering, and Business Administration. These programs focus on advanced research, innovation, and academic excellence, preparing students to contribute original knowledge and lead in their disciplines. The PhD programs emphasize rigorous research and critical thinking skills.
